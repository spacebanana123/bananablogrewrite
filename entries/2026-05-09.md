# BEAUTIFUL CHAOS
BEAUTIFUL CHAOS is an extended play by KATSEYE. I think this delves back into hyperpop and I personally don't like it that much. It feels a little bit like my mind is too slow to keep up. I imagine that for a type of person that enjoys thinking at a million miles an hour, this is the kind of album that would appeal to them, but its just not form for me.
On a more positive note, I do think the extended play as a whole integrates well together with each song forming sufficient diversity to make listening enjoyable, but also having a sort of central them. M.I.A. is definitely a song that you could bop you head to on its catchy beat. On the whole though, I think I'll give it a 6.5 out of 10. Not bad, but a little much for me.
