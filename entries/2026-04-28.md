# Nostalgia in Times Square
Nostalgia in Times Square by Mingus Big Band is a collection of jazz songs that I don't mesh with. Unfortunately for it, contexts in which I listen to music seem to hurt the benefits of this collection of songs. 
I primarily listen to music during the completion of other tasks. I assume other people might listen to music in the same way that I read books, as a primary focus to consume art. To those folks, I would whole heartedly recomend Nostalgia in Times Square as a good album. For me though, the songs lack neat patterns that can hold my focus, but instead often detract from my ability to focus. In this way, I feel that the songs miss the boat on what I want from music. 
To be sure, I feel that this is a good collection of songs. The soft tones of Duke Ellington's Sound of Love stand out to me as an excellent piece that can stick with you, but I think I will slot it at 5 out of 10. Not bad, but not for me.
