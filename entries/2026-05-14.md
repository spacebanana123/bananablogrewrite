# A Matter of Time
review_score 8
A Matter of Time is a jazz(ish?) album by Laufey. I definitely enjoyed listening to this one overall and found it a nice time. It definitely fuses a kind of pop aesthetic with jazz that enables a really interesting texture to the music that I haven't heard before. It combines to create a compelling album that also includes potent lyrics that makes a for a nice listening experience. 
I don't really have much more to add. This was enjoyable. I feel like I should pad this out with some commentary, but, to be honest, I just don't have anything here. I also don't exactly have the music knowledge to describe what exactly makes it "jazz(ish)" as I put it. It just feels like it. 
With all of that waffling over, I put this album at a solid 8 out of 10. I liked it and I threw Castle in Hollywood into my main playlist.
