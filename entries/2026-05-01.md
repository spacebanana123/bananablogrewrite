# A Love Supreme
review_score 6
A Love Supreme by John Coltrane is a haunting album. I don't like the fact that there is different sounds in the <a href ="/2026-04-30">left and right audio channels</a>, but I do like the jazz from this album. 
This album is very short. It is just four total songs that have an overall theme about god. I definitely think that if you like jazz you should listen to this one, and even if you don't you should still listen to it just to experience it.
I don't think I will add any of the songs from this album to my main playlist because I don't think I can really deal with the mixing, but I would recommend it to others. Overall this is a 6 out of 10.
