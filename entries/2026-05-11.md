Missed days count: 2. I was tired yesterday and decided to skip it since another missed day doesn't really matter after losing perfect. 
Homework by Daft Punk is my first venture into the groups music and I have to say that I don't really like it all that much. I get how some people can like it, so I don't think the music is actively bad, but the album has a really frustrating property of having repeating sounds that slightly vary in ways that are annoying to me. It feels, to me, much more like variance for variance's sake rather than building up to something interesting on its own. 
I don't quite really like the overall texture of the music that feels too abrasive for me. Even though I understand that abrasiveness is part of the point, part of the point of this is me figuring out what I do and do not like. 
Either way, this gets a 6 out of 10. It was fine and I wouldn't demand it get skipped in a car ride. Certainly not in the main playlist.
