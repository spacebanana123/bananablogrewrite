# I Miss You Already + I Haven’t Left Yet
I Miss You Already + I Haven't Left Yet is an album of songs that are depressing and I like them. What that says about me is anyone's guess, but Gemini from this album has managed to make it into my main playlist. 
I would say that there is nice beat that sits under generally soft vocal that generate a vibe of both sadness and comfort in that sadness. 
The vocal peaks then are moments that I could totally imagine myself belting out loud in car with friends or at a party. To me at least, that elevates this album to something that you would want on rotation. It's an expression of catchiness for me. 
Overall, a very good 8.5 out of 10. The album as a whole can have moments were it feels the subject matter gets a little tired, but it doesn't dock it that much and the overall high quality can carry it over the line.
