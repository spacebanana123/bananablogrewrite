# pullup to busan 4 morE hypEr summEr it’s gonna bE a fuckin moviE
"pullup to busan 4 morE hypEr summEr it’s gonna bE a fuckin moviE" by Effie was very good good! My understanding is that is influence by KPop, but not of the KPop scene. To me, the album has an overwhelmingly positive vibe, although I can't understand any of the Korean in the songs. It feels, in a word, nice. 
The album covers an impressive array of different sounds and feelings in its short run time that could almost be considered an extended play. I suspect if there was a wikipedia article about this album, they would call it an EP. 
Either way, I initially assumed that I wouldn't like this album. Being described as "hyperpop" didn't really seem like my scene, but Effie pulls of an impressive level of catchiness in songs that I can't understand.  It truly feels like I want to bop my head to every song in this album (with the possible exception of thankie thankie, but I appreciate its presence as a closer).
Overall, a very high 9 out of 10. MORE HYPER made it to my main playlist and LET'S FIND A GOOD MANAGER is also a standout song. 
I have noticed my ratings these past few have been very high. Maybe it's time for a recalibration.
