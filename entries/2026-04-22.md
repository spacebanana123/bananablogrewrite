# Takanaka
Takanaka by Masayoshi Takanaka is pretty good. And that's about it. 
I listened while doing some other work in the morning and I found that it was pretty decent. None of the songs I felt were stand out nor particularly called to me, but for an overall set of pretty good songs that have a nice vibe to them, I can't complain. Will I seek this album out? Probably not. Will I avoid it? No. If I ever need to lock in, but a kinda chill lock in, this seems like a pretty good set of songs.
Overall, a solid 6 out of 10.
