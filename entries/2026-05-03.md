# KARMA
review_score 7.5
KARMA by Stray Kids is pretty good. Overall I don't think it is particularly standout in terms of the KPop albums that I have listened to, certainly not quite having the character that <a href="/2026-04-27">pull up to busan</a> did, but it does solid work. 
For me at least, it meets the metric of having a set of vibes that I could imagine myself singing aloud or bopping my head to. In fact, I was primarily studying while listening to this album, but I still ended up bobbing my head up and down as I listened to this one. It is solid and does the job well, even if I don't think it stands much apart from the crowd in my mind. 
Over all a 7.5 out of 10 and CEREMONY was my favorite which merited an inclusion in my main playlist.
