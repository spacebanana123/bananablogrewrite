# Hades
Hades is an OST, which I recently learned is short for Original Soundtrack. Darren Korb created the Hades OST and to me this is very good study music. Almost all of the songs don't have any lyrics which critically important for me when I am studying. Any lyrics are distracting and hurt my ability to focus on the task at hand. 
The other important attribute for me is that the songs really have energy. Continual energy that pushes studying forward. Its a vibe that I think is best is described by dynamism. All of these Hades songs push forward is a way that is fitting of the game itself.
I haven't played Hades, but pushing forwards against constraining forces seems like an apt description for the games "rogue-lite" nature. 
Either way, none of these songs make it into my main playlist and this album is tough to rate, but I end up giving it a solid 8 out of 10. Out of Tartarus, The Painful Way, From Olympus, Through Asphodel, and The Unseen Ones all make it into my new study playlist.
