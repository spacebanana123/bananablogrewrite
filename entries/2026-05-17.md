# Within Dreams
review_score 5
Within Dreams by Feels Like Heaven is a very aggressive album, and I don't like it. I have a feeling that amongst a certain crowd, this is a very good album that is deserving of much praise. I am not in that crowd.
A few points that annoy me personally:
1. Different left and right audio channels. I have a feeling that Feels Like Heaven is doing this on purpose, but I don't understand the point and it ends up just distracting from the music. 
2. Its a lot. If you like a lot, then you'll probably like this one. I don't really and I think this one definitely  hits the nail on the head  of how to be a lot. The music is loud in and in your face throughout the album.
3. To be honest, the album mostly sounds the same. Perhaps someone who is more of an expert would be able to tell you what the nuances are, but to me its mostly drowned out in a wave of sound. 
Does any of this make the album bad per say? No. It doesn't. Does all of this make it bad for me? Yes it does.  5 out of 10. Missed days count is also at 3 now. I skipped yesterday because I got busy doing other stuff.
