Minisode 3: Tomorrow is an "extended play"? I learned a new term today! Its shorter than an album but longer than a single. Learning new terms is always intriguing to me because they often are a reflection of the values and ambitions of the people that make up that group.
Anyways, my initiaial impression is that the morse code bit is interesting but would get old quick with repeated listens to this album. To me, it's definitely novel and interesting, but doesn't really have any staying power to me.
This was definitely a different venture for me and the use of korean language that I have zero understanding of makes this occupy a similar space as study songs that I listen to in order to focus and not get distracted by lyrics. 
I don't think any of the songs will make it into my main playlist, but its definitely an album that I would keep in mind for the future. 7 out of 10.
