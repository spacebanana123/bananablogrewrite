# Nevermind
review_score 5
I'm trying something interesting to listen to a new album every day. My music tastes have generally gotten pretty stale, so pushing myself to expand my tastes is the goal. I'm sourcing these recommendations from friends mostly and writing out these reviews whenever I can. I hope I don't sound too pretenious about it, but these are my genuine (at the time) thoughts. 
Today was Nevermind by Nirvana. To be honest, I don't like it. I can see how others would like it, but to me it feels a little too hectic and loud (I know it's expected stylistically from Nirvana). I, to be honest, expected that I wouldn't really like it but interesting to have it confirmed. Smells like teen spirit is definitely my favorite of the album, but it's just not for me. It isn't bad, but it's not a fit for me
Overall its a 5 out of 10. I can tell it can be good, but isn't for me. It won't end up in any of my playlists.
