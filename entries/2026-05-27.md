# Crying Laughing Loving Lying
Crying Laughing Loving Lying by Labi Siffre is an album that I enjoyed a lot. Texturally I feel this album is very close to country. I think I feel this way because the album primarily focuses on a single singer and a central guitar. This is very cool to me as well, because I don't feel irritated by this album in the same way that I felt about <a href="/5-20-2026">Dandelion</a>. 
I think this is sufficiently different from country music that I don't get that oversaturation annoyance that I get from other country albums. It really feels like something newer and interesting that makes it possible for me to enjoy it more. 
Overall, I think this albums fills the kind of spot that country might for other people. The songs on the whole are pretty chill and enjoyable without having super complicated instrumentals. I hope I can find more albums similar to it that can fill this space without being oversaturating. I gave the album a very good 9 out of 10 and added My Song to my main playlist.
