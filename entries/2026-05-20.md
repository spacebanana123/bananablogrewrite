# Dandelion
review_score 6
Dandelion by Ella Langley is pretty decent, but to be honest, I think it suffers most from the fact that I don't really care. I think country has become a style of music that I perceive as generic and boring even when it isn't. This is probably because I personally think the texture of most country songs are similar to most other country songs, in a way that makes me have a hard time telling the songs as different.
I think this issue expresses itself as I don't really know when one song ended and another began in the album. I will admit that I was doing some other non-related programming while listening, but even then, the songs blended together so much that I didn't have any response to most of the songs. 
I think country in general will probably have a hard time breaking out of this perception for me. I'm not sure what variation in country music could really break up the sameness I feel. Overall I give this album a 6 out of 10. It wasn't unpleasant or anything, but it really wasn't anything.
