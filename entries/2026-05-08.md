# The Great Divide
The Great Divide is an album I now love. This is amazing. This is cool and great. I like pretty much everything about it besides the distracting mixing that doesn't have the left and right channels be the same. 
Despite that, the sheer quality of this album makes me willing to let that slide. Either way, I really like both the vocals and the instrumentals to this album and it covers an excellent amount of ground. 
Overall a very good 8.5 out of 10 for me. I liked Deny Deny Deny the most and you should listen to it too.
