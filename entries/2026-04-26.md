# Wishbone 
Wishbone by Conan Gray is pretty enjoyable. It is yet another series of songs about love and contains a decent amount about being sad because of hardships in love. Similarly there are generally softer vocals that overlay consistent beats that escalate to peaks that I could imagine belting out. 
There definitely is a warmer tint to this album than the previous one. Generally more upbeat and positive, even in the songs that are sad, it seems less sad than <a href="/4-25-2026">the previous album</a>. The songs have a heavier centralization on warmer tones. 
Overall, I think I would put at a very good 8.7 out of 10. I do think I generally think I prefer this one over my previous album, but its a very close call. I added Romeo to my main playlist.
