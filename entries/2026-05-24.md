# Kind Of Blue
review_score 8.5
Kind Of Blue by Miles Davis is a very famous jazz album. Arguably it is the most famous jazz album ever. That hasn't really transferred to my personal opinion on it though. 
I feel that I don't really have a connection to the songs that I feel like the lyric can create. It's interesting that I feel some people can have a really deep connection to the mood or spirit of a song without lyrics, but listening to the songs on their own, it just doesn't quite do it for me. 
On a technical level, I actually do kind of like Miles Davis having two audio channels for left and right, but I also kind of hate it. I think it emulate the atmosphere of being in a the midst of a jazz band very well, but it also irritates me in a way that I've explained a lot before. 
Overall, this is good album at 8.5 out of 10. I enjoyed listening to it and added All Blues to my playlist, but I don't think I will be looking at lyric-less jazz for much longer.
