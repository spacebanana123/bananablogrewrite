the record by boygenius feels fine? I don't know. Perhaps I'm missing something, cuz it got a Grammy. In fac, it got three Grammys in best Alternative Music Album, Best Rock Song, and Best Rock Performance. Maybe in 5ish years or whenever I embark on the project of relistening to these albums to examine how my music tastes have changed I will conclude that this is good music. But that isn't me right now and future me doesn't exist.
I'm not quite sure how to describe my own personal ambivalence towards this album. There is nothing about it that makes me overtly dislike it, but I generally don't have any reactions to it. Perhaps this suffers from an interesting corollary to the problems that much of Kendrick Lamar's albums have for me. I don't have the richness of life experience that can make me feel compelled. Or at least my life experience and perhaps the focus of my life right now isn't the subject matter that the record primarily discusses. 
Overall, I don't think I lost anything by listening to it, but I never felt particularly like I wanted to relisten. Its like one of those songs that if someone else put on the shared playlist I would just scroll past. Not for any of it's own faults, but mostly because it isn't for me. 6 out of 10.
