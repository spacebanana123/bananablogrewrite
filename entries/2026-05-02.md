# I Against I
review_score 7
I Against I by Bad Brains is a recommendation from a friend. My friend did say that they weren't sure if I would like the songs, but I would find it interesting. I would say that I certainly do find it interesting. 
You can certainly feel a kind of cold war era influence from this album and a distinctly punk style that also incorporates other elements. As a whole I'm not super sure what I want to do with the album. The loud volume of this album doesn't really feel like something I like, but part of me all feels compelled by the songs and overall vibe. Its a strange place to be in, but it definitely makes me feel more conflicted than anything. 
Its certainly abrasive but in a way that I kind of like? I give it a 7 out of 10 and honestly might revisit this one later. It seems like an interesting benchmark to see how my taste changes with time.
