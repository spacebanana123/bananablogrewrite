# Sunburn
review_score 6
Sunburn by Dominic Fike is pretty nifty, but kinda boring to me. Maybe its just that I'm particularly burnt out on love songs or that I just aren't in the mood for them, but I don't like it. Like these songs are clearly good. The lyrics are particularly smooth and feel good with a smooth texture to them, but I just can't care about the struggles and life of it. 
Maybe in some ways it is just that I don't have the kind of understanding of when I would want these songs. It doesn't feel quite like hype songs that could make you super energized. It doesn't feel like interesting commentary or reflections or response to some kind of tragedy. It operates in the messsy middle that makes me feel more tired than anything else. 
Overall, I think this rating is in all likelihood more harsh than Sunburn actually deserves, but I think its definitely a reflection of my own sense of tiredness in someway. 6 out of 10.
