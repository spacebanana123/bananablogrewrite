# The Human Fear
review_score 8.5
The Human Fear by Franz Ferdinand is surprisingly good. I didn't come in with many expectations in terms of what I thought this album was going to be as a I don't listen to Franz Ferdiand much. I mostly thought it would be an interesting venture into a band that I don't listen much to. 
I certainly enjoyed my listening experience. There are points in this album where it can feel a little repetitive especially with Hooked and Black Eyelashes with me. To be clear, those two songs aren't that similar, but they individual feel a little repetitive and drag on. 
I think Franz Ferdinand sounds similar to <a href="/2026-04-30">Geese</a> but in a way that I like more. The vibes match me more which, while entirely subjective, does help a lot. I personally thought that build it up was really catchy with a core beat that felt compelling to me and made me perk up from studying. While that isn't entirely a good thing when you are trying to focus, it really does illustrate how powerful an effect that song had. 
Overall a good 8.5 out of 10 that makes me feel interesting in looking at some of the other discography from Franz Ferdinand. I added Build It Up to my main album.
