# Dark Side of the Moon
review_score 9
Listened to The Dark Side of the Moon today. I really enjoyed this one. I think stylistically it really matches more with what I tend to like. The beats are pretty sick. I listened to this one with the lyrics open and I realized that many of the songs don't actually have that many words in them, which was pretty nifty. Favorite was the Great Gig in the Sky, although I already listen to that one a lot since its part of the main playlist I listen to.
I also found the openning to this album interesting. Since I listen to albums in the listed order from first song to last and then write out my thoughts, the openning was not what I expected given Great Gig in the Sky. I still thought it served as a good openner, but it certainly wouldn't make it into my main playlist. 
Overall, no modifications to my main playlist, but I already had songs in that playlist, so pretty much a moot point. I rated this 9 out of 10. That for me is a pretty stellar rating.
